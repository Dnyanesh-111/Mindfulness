Meditation Courses

Mindfulness offers advanced online meditation courses to help people start, stabilize and deepen their practice through profound and authentic meditation guidance. Let us guide you with our unique meditation training methods to create positive, lasting change in your life.

Are you ready to do what it takes for positive transformation? Are you interested in making meditation part of your life? Mindworks is for you. Our outstanding online courses offer genuine meditation guidance featuring the highest quality instructions available anywhere. Great meditation teachers and influential pathfinders from around the planet participate in Mindworks’ powerful online learning experiences. Mindworks offers advanced mindfulness meditation training in a virtual meditation classroom that will help you discover your mind’s potential, bring joy and fearlessness into your life, and share happiness with others to change the world for the better.

Wondering where to start? Right now we’re offering Level 1: Fundamentals absolutely free. Our outstanding introductory meditation course is the first stage of our 9-level Mindworks Journey. There is no better way to learn to meditate and practice consistently on your own. Mindworks Journey, our core mindfulness and awareness meditation course, helps meditators of all levels build consistency and ease through progressive guided meditation training. Join us and cultivate genuine balance and well-being in your life.

Our students love our courses!

Mindworks courses are designed to help you achieve consistency and depth in your meditation so you can carry your practice—and its benefits—into your life. You can also give the gift of mindfulness to a friend. We provide certificates for every completed course.
